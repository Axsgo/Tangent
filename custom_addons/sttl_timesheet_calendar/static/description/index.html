OVERVIEW

A Calendar Time-sheet module is a type of timesheet that is organized and displayed in a calendar format. Instead of using a traditional table-based layout this module uses a calendar view to record and track the time spent on different activities or tasks.
Businesses often uses drag and drop software to optimize all of their needs. Traditional methods such as Excel tend to be clunky and inefficient.

FEATURES

Here are some key aspects and benefits of the timesheet calendar feature:



These Application provides a Calendar view that lets you select the time slot to fill the timesheet in calendar view. These view lets you fill all the details of Timesheet. Time period, event titles, tasks activities and descriptions entered in calendar view the spreadsheet. Once those settings are configured, you can sync events and view your activities on a dashboard.


Supported: v14 15 16

WHY DO WE NEED TIMESHEET CALENDAR IN OUR BUSINESS?


  • More Visibility: Project time tracking offers real-time benefits and visibility into projects - whether they are organizational-wide projects, departmental projects or projects managed by inter-departmental teams.
  • Accurate: Calendar timesheets benefit in the accuracy with which people manage their timesheets, which is less prone to error than list view entry.
  • Simple & Easy: A Calendar timesheet is simple, easy, fast
  • Increased Productivity: Increased productivity and also reduce overtime costs. This helps them to maintain their budget for the project and reduce costs.
  • Identify Improvement Areas: It gives business owners a better understanding of the daily activities of their employees. It quickly identifies the improvement areas by doing tasks viz. evaluate the skills of the employees, provides an overview of team tasks and efforts and finally take the necessary steps to make improvements.
